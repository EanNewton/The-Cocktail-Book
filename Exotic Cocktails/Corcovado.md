# Corcovado

---

## Ingredients

* 1 measure blue Curacao
* 1 measure tequila
* 1 measure Drambuie
* Lemonade to top up

---

## Steps

1.  Shake ingredients well with ice and strain into a highball glass full of crushed ice. Top with lemonade and decorate with a slice of lemon or fresh lime and 2 thin straws.
