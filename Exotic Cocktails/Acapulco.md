# Acapulco

---

## Ingredients

* 1 measure light rum
* 1/4 measure lime juice
* 1/2 measure Cointreau or Triple Sec
* 1/2 egg white
* 1/2 tsp sugar
* Soda to top up

---

## Steps

1.  Shake ingredients well with ice and pour into a highball glass. Top with soda and decorate with 2 fresh mint leaves and 2 thin straws.
