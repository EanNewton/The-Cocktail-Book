# Prairie Oyster I

---

## Ingredients

* 1 egg yolk
* Cayenne pepper
* 1 dash of Worcestershire suace
* 1 dash of Tabasco
* 1 tsp wine vinegar
* 1 measure brandy

---

## Steps

1.  Mix the ingredients very carefully in a rocks glass, ensuring that you do not break the yolk. Drink in one gulp. For Prairie Oyster II, see 'Non-Alcoholic Cocktails'.
